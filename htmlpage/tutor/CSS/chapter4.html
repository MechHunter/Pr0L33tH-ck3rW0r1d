The diagram of doom!!!

this diagram shows where padding, borders and margins go. Lets go through it together!

the positions

as we can see on the diagram, there are some words on the top, bottom, left and right being named respectivly. These are the positions. in a css style sheet, yes you can type padding: 10px; which creates 10 picels of padding all around the content, but if you want to change individual sides, you use the positions. For example padding-left: 10px;

the styling

on this diagram we also see the stylings being margin, border and padding, content is just the content, this diagram shows you where they are located and that is what you will be changing with each line of code.
